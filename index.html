WAR ON THE STAGE.

John Kadivane is not just a rally driver — he is a brand, a community builder, and a champion in every lane he enters. The 2023 Kenya National Autocross Rally Champion (Open Class) and founder of Umoja Racing, one of Kenya's most exciting grassroots motorsport teams built on the philosophy of unity and relentless commitment, John has become one of the most recognizable faces in Kenyan motorsport.

Behind the wheel of his Craftworks Automotive-backed machine, he has collected podiums across Autocross and Rallycross, and most recently served as Competitor Relations Officer (CRO) at the 2025 WRC Safari Rally — a role that placed him at the very heart of world-class rally operations on African soil, bridging the gap between elite international teams and race officials. Off the stages, John serves as Secretary of the 254 Motorsport Club and navigated competitively in 2024, further cementing his all-round expertise in the sport.

In 2025, he added another chapter to his story — podium finishes in RX, a standout performance at the Fun Race in Machakos, and the ultimate recognition when he was honoured as MEA Best Personality of the Year 2025. His ifluence extends well beyond motorsport. With over 10 years in business development, a decade of empowering creatives through technology, and a background spanning forensics, cyber security, and IT solutions, John brings an unmatched combination of intellect and raw driving talent. Partnering with John Kadivane means aligning your brand with a proven champion, a growing team in Umoja Racing, and a personality that resonates deeply with Kenya's passionate motorsport community and beyond. The stages are set — the question is, will your brand be on the car?
